Kuma

Type:

Water

Traits:

  • Calm
  • Disciplined
  • Consistent
  • Worries Alot

Personalities:

  • Critical Thinking
    • They like to ask for opinions when solving a problem
    • They solve problems very methodically
    • They are careful
    • They are slow learners but can retain information much longer
    • Advice: Interleave your resources, Teach for retention, Connect your ideas.
  • Motivation
    • They have incredible endurance
    • They are very consistent
    • They are less sensitive to stimulation
    • Nothing really excites or bothers them
    • But they tend to go slow.
    • Advice: Don't linger to much on a problem so as not to slow down your progress
  • Time Management
    • They are most productive at night or early in the morning
    • They are good with consistency
    • Advice: Implement a Morning and Night routine and Balanced Nutrition.
  • Stress Management
    • They are very self-critical
    • This make them prone to depression
    • They worry about lacking behind
    • They often compare their progress to others
    • Advice: Practice Mindfulness, Gratitude, and Enjoy the journey.
