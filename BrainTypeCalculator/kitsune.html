Kitsune

Type:

Wind

Traits:

  • Charismatic
  • Creative
  • Excitable
  • Very Sensitive
  • Curious
  • Spontaneous

Personalities:

  • Critical Thinking
    • They are dynamic
    • They are energetic and eager to take information as much and as fast as possible
    • They prefer to try first then think later
    • They are fast learners but also fast forgetters
    • Advice: Space out your reviews, Identify weaknesses and Quiz yourself don't be so hasty to move on.
  • Motivation
    • They are easily absorbed into their work
    • Their curiosity is unpredictable, they tend to bounce from one interest to another
    • They move to another interests so quickly that they don't master them.
    • They are fast learners and can multitask
    • Advice: Stick to a goal and achieve it before moving to another goal
  • Time Management
    • They are most when they follow their own interests throughout the day
    • They do very well without a strict schedule
    • They thrive when they have a list of projects to work on
    • Advice: Make a mix of your interest with your goal e.g Pick a study subject, a habit to build and an hobby. And when you get bored of one move to the next one.
  • Stress Management
    • They feel stressed when they overthink
    • They get stressed when criticised
    • They worry about little things and overthink too much
    • They often exagerate consequences of most situations
    • They are easily prone to anxiety
    • Advice: Practice Mindfulness, Live in the present, don't dwell on your past, Practice focus.
