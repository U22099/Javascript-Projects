Tora

Type:

Fire

Traits:

  • Ambitious
  • Highly Passionate
  • Goal-Oriented
  • Relentless
  • Never gives up

Personalities:

  • Critical Thinking
    • They do whatever it takes to get what they want
    • They are systematic and organized in problem solving
    • They are strategic
    • They develop curiosity and use it as fuel to achieve their goals
    • Advice: Use clear learning objectives, Structure your knowledge and Learn key concepts first
  • Motivation
    • They are naturally highly motivated in any goal they pursue
    • They have high passion for what they do and never give up
    • But they tend to over stress themselves in achieving a goal
    • They want to achieve things fast without taking a rest
    • Advice: Take a rest often, Do not overwork yourself.
  • Time Management
    • They are most productive between the hours of 8am to 6pm
    • They are quick to climb the career ladder
    • Advice: Make use of Calenders, Time Blockings And to-do list to enhance productivity, And don't forget to schedule on breaks.
  • Stress Management
    • They have strong emotions
    • They feel stress when others don't seem to get along with them
    • They get angry when hungry
    • These stress expressions comes out strong in team settings
    • Advice: Practice Mindfulness, Deep Breathing and Empathy to regain your focus.
